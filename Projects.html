  • README: This a file related to Autism Detection Where A adversial Cartoon Movies are displayed and after analysis the sentiment judging the child has either Autism disorder or not

    • Abstract:Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a complex neurodevelopmental condition characterized by challenges in social interaction, communication, and repetitive behaviors. Early detection and intervention are crucial for improving outcomes for children with ASD. This project aims to develop an AI-based system to detect potential signs of autism by analyzing a child's facial expressions, particularly their smiling behaviors, while they watch cartoon videos designed to elicit smiles and laughter. The system leverages machine learning and computer vision techniques to track and analyze facial expressions captured through a webcam. Using a dataset of cartoon videos known for their humor and engagement, the AI model assesses the frequency, duration, and intensity of the child's smiles. By comparing these metrics to established behavioral patterns in neurotypical children and those diagnosed with ASD, the system can identify atypical responses that may indicate the presence of autism. This approach offers a non-invasive, enjoyable, and potentially scalable method for early autism detection. The project not only provides valuable insights into the nuanced behaviors associated with ASD but also highlights the potential of integrating AI technologies in pediatric healthcare for early diagnosis and intervention strategies.

    Keywords: Autism Spectrum Disorder, facial expressions, smiling behaviors, cartoon videos, machine learning, computer vision, early detection.

    Requirements:1.python 2.streamlit 3.opencv 4.sentiment analysis
